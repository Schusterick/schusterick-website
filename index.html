Family & Life

Off the course.

Home is Dalton, Georgia — my wife Latricia and our two kids, Liam and Lily. They're my whole world, and they're the reason I came off the road.

I don't travel to compete anymore. I'm home every day now, present as a dad and a husband, and grateful I still get to build in the sport I love as CEO of Prodigy — with the best support system I could ask for behind me.
